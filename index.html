Cab Booking Management System
Pre-requisites: Java, Data Structures, Netbeans, Java Swing components

Yagi Uda Antenna of 4 elements
Pre-requisites: Waveguides & Antennas, CADFEKO

A Yagi Uda Antenna was built on the basis of the design made on the software CADFEKO. Antenna specifications, resonant frequency, and basic construction required for the hardware was first designed on CADFEKO and the hardware was then built.

FM Transmitter
Pre-requisites: Electronic Devices & Circuits, Passive & Active Components

A short-range FM transmitter is a low-power FM radio transmitter that broadcasts a signal from a portable audio device (such as an MP3 player) to a standard FM radio. These transmitters plug into the device's headphone jack and then broadcast the signal over an FM broadcast band frequency, so that it can be picked up by any nearby radio. It was made using transistors, power amplifiers and resistors.

Line & Edge Follower Bot
Pre-requisites: Arduino

A robotic vehicle developed using Arduino which follows about a particular drawn line ( either light or dark) and does not stray from the track. For line detection logic, two IR Sensors were used, which consist of IR LED and Photodiode. They are placed in a reflective way i.e. side – by – side so that whenever they come in to proximity of a reflective surface, the light emitted by IR LED will be detected by Photo diode and thus the bot will move along a line.
